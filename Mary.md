# Mary

A [[javascript]] Chrome/Vivaldi [[browser-extension]] for granular per-domain cookie management and tracking.

**Repo:** `~/Git/Mary`
**Themes:** [[javascript]], [[browser-extension]]

Mary tracks which domains set cookies as you browse and lets you decide per-domain whether to keep or auto-delete them. Logs cookie metadata (no values) in a three-tier hierarchy: visited domain, cookie domain, individual cookies. Manifest V3, no framework dependencies.
