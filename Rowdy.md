# Rowdy

A text-to-speech service built around the Piper neural TTS engine, with a custom DSL for scripting speech, a REST API, and a web frontend — planned in six clearly-staged phases.

**Repo:** `~/Git/Rowdy`
**Themes:** (TTS)

Plan structure is unusually crisp for this corpus: `phase-1-setup.md`, `phase-2-dsl.md`, `phase-3-piper.md`, `phase-4-api.md`, `phase-5-frontend.md`, `phase-6-testing.md`, plus a top-level `PLAN.md`, `DOCS.md` and `DEPLOY.md`. `piper-tts-server/` is the actual Piper integration subfolder. **Status:** plan-heavy, but the presence of `piper-tts-server/` with real content suggests phases 1–3 are at least partly implemented; phases 4–6 (API, frontend, testing) are unclear without reading the individual phase docs.
