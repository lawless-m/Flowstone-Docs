# MonkeyTamper

A collection of Tampermonkey userscripts — dark mode themes for eBay and Hacker News, plus a neon scrollbar override.

**Repo:** `~/Git/MonkeyTamper`
**Themes:** [[javascript]], [[browser-extension]], [[Gogs]]

Three userscripts: dark mode for eBay (all regional domains), dark mode for Hacker News, and a cyberpunk neon scrollbar applied globally. Injected at document-start to prevent white flashes, with MutationObservers for dynamic content. Auto-updates from [[Gogs]] on [[dw.ramsden-international.com]].
