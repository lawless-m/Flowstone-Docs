# ExcelMacros

VBA macros for Excel automation — seasonal order form generation, budget processing, and product image embedding.

**Repo:** `~/Git/ExcelMacros`
**Themes:** [[VBA]], [[Gogs]]

A collection of Excel VBA modules for Ramsden/Nisa business workflows: generating customer-specific Easter and Xmas order form workbooks from master templates, processing annual budget data, and embedding product images fetched by EAN code from [[dw.ramsden-international.com]]. Hosted on [[Gogs]].
