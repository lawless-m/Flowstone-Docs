# EM-Document-Scripts

VBScript document templates for the [[ExportMaster]] print system — purchase orders, invoices, packing lists, and more.

**Repo:** `~/Git/EM-Document-Scripts`

A collection of 100+ VBScript print scripts that customise document generation in [[ExportMaster]] via its E3Ax COM API. Covers region-specific variants (UK, EU, Spain, Portugal), pallet deal logic, NISA product handling, and multi-warehouse operations. Hosted on [[Gogs]] at [[Dw.RamsdenInternational.Com]].
