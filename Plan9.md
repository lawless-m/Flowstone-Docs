# Plan9

Plan 9 / 9front homages, implementations, and infrastructure in this corpus. Matt's affection for Bell Labs' Plan 9 shows up directly in 9P protocol work and obliquely in projects that borrow Plan 9 ideas — like Venti's content-addressed storage design surfacing inside a block-storage system.

## Members

- [[Crabbit]] — userspace WireGuard peer exposed as a Plan 9 `/net` filesystem over 9P
- [[Nawin]] — .NET 9P2000 client/server library for Plan 9 and 9front file servers
- [[VoE]] — "Venti over Ethernet", a block-storage homage to Plan 9's Venti content-addressed archive
